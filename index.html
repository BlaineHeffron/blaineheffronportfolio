About

Background

I run Blaine Heffron Consulting. I help teams fix clunky workflows, connect the right systems, and build software that matches the job.

I have a PhD in physics from the University of Tennessee, where I worked on the PROSPECT neutrino experiment at Oak Ridge. That shows up now in machine learning, scientific computing, and software where the technical details actually matter.

Recent work includes StockForge, Songcraft, Stellar tooling, and custom software for teams buried in process.

I'm most useful when the work is messy and someone needs to sort out what should be fixed, automated, or rebuilt.
